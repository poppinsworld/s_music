# Design System

## Base Foundation
- **Framework**: Flutter Material 3 (with deep customizations).
- **Style**: Futuristic, AMOLED-optimized, premium neon aesthetics.
- **Components**: Glassmorphism, dynamic gradients, neon borders.

## Colors (Initial Strategy)
- Primary: Deep neon purple/blue.
- Background: True black (AMOLED).
- Surface: Translucent glass elements.

## Reusable Widget Foundation
Located in `lib/shared/`:
- **Glass Container**: A translucent box with background blur and subtle borders.
- **Player Card**: A visual representation of currently playing media.
- **Bottom Navigation**: Custom navigation shell with glass aesthetics.
- **Reusable Button**: Animated, styled buttons for common actions.
